# EarnMart Bot - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** October 29, 2025

## 1. Introduction

EarnMart ("the Bot") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and your rights regarding your information. By using the Bot, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

## 2. Data Controller

The Bot operator is responsible for data collection and processing. The Bot is operated for use within Discord servers and is not affiliated with Discord Inc.

## 3. Data We Collect

### 3.1 Discord User Information
We collect and store the following information from Discord:
- **User ID**: Your unique Discord user identifier
- **Server ID (Guild ID)**: The Discord server where you use the Bot
- **Display Name**: Your Discord username or server nickname (for display purposes only)

### 3.2 Economy Data
We collect and maintain:
- **Account Balance**: Your virtual currency balance (in ðŸª™ coins)
- **Transaction History**: Records of earnings, purchases, and transfers
- **Daily Reward Status**: Last reward claim time and streak information
- **Online Reward Status**: Last online reward claim time

### 3.3 Marketplace Data
We collect and store:
- **Product Listings**: Products you list for sale, including name, description, price, and category
- **Digital Content**: Any digital content you provide (files, links, codes, instructions)
- **Purchase History**: Records of products you've bought or sold
- **Seller Information**: Your identity as a seller of products

### 3.4 Inventory Data
We collect and maintain:
- **Purchased Items**: Digital products you've purchased
- **Item Details**: Content and metadata associated with your purchased items

### 3.5 Usage Data
We may collect:
- **Command Usage**: Which Bot commands you use
- **Timestamps**: When you interact with the Bot
- **Error Information**: Technical data when errors occur

## 4. Data We Do NOT Collect

The Bot does **not** collect or store:
- Personal identifying information (real name, email, phone number, address)
- Messages sent in Discord servers
- Friendship lists or relationships
- Any data outside of Discord server contexts
- Payment information (transactions handled by Discord only)
- IP addresses or device information

## 5. How We Use Your Data

### 5.1 Core Functionality
Your data is used to:
- Maintain your account balance and economy data
- Process transactions and marketplace purchases
- Display your products and listings
- Track daily and online rewards
- Provide economy statistics and user information

### 5.2 Service Improvement
We may use aggregated, anonymized data to:
- Analyze Bot performance and usage patterns
- Identify bugs and technical issues
- Improve user experience and features
- Generate server-wide statistics

### 5.3 Legal Compliance
We may disclose data if required by:
- Applicable laws or regulations
- Lawful government requests
- Protection of our rights or safety
- Prevention of fraud or illegal activity

### 5.4 We Will NOT Use Data For:
- Targeted advertising or marketing
- Selling data to third parties
- Creating user profiles beyond functionality
- Any purpose other than Bot operation

## 6. Data Retention

### 6.1 Retention Policy
- **Active Users**: Data is retained as long as you use the Bot in a server
- **Inactive Users**: Data may be retained indefinitely unless deletion is requested
- **Deleted Servers**: Data associated with deleted Discord servers may be deleted
- **Removed Users**: If you leave a server, your data persists in that server until manually deleted

### 6.2 Transaction History
- Transaction records are maintained for the lifetime of your account
- Purchase history helps maintain marketplace integrity

### 6.3 Backup Data
- Database backups may retain deleted data temporarily
- Backups are deleted according to standard database retention practices

## 7. Data Security

### 7.1 Security Measures
We implement:
- Secure database connections with encryption
- Access controls limiting who can view data
- Regular security reviews and updates
- Password protection for database access
- Standard industry security practices

### 7.2 Limitations
- We cannot guarantee 100% security against all threats
- Discord API security is managed by Discord Inc.
- Users are responsible for securing their Discord accounts

### 7.3 Data Breaches
In the event of a data breach, we will:
- Investigate the incident promptly
- Take corrective action to prevent future breaches
- Notify affected users when appropriate
- Comply with applicable notification laws

## 8. Third-Party Sharing

### 8.1 Data Sharing
We **do not share** your data with:
- Third-party advertisers or marketers
- Social media platforms
- Data brokers or analytics companies
- Any unaffiliated third parties

### 8.2 Discord Integration
- The Bot integrates with Discord's API
- Discord's privacy practices apply to Discord-provided data
- Refer to Discord's Privacy Policy for their data practices

### 8.3 Server Administrators
- Server administrators can access aggregated server statistics
- Individual user data is only visible to the user and Bot operator
- Administrators cannot access other users' private transactions or balances

## 9. Your Privacy Rights

### 9.1 Right to Access
You have the right to:
- Request what personal data we hold about you
- Receive a copy of your data in human-readable format
- Contact the Bot operator to request your information

### 9.2 Right to Deletion
You have the right to:
- Request deletion of your account data
- Remove specific products or listings
- Have your data deleted upon leaving a server
- Request permanent deletion of all associated data

**How to Request Deletion:**
Contact the Bot operator with your request. Deletions will be processed within a reasonable timeframe.

### 9.3 Right to Correction
You have the right to:
- Correct inaccurate data
- Update product information
- Modify product descriptions or pricing

### 9.4 Right to Restrict Processing
You have the right to:
- Limit how your data is used
- Opt out of specific data collection practices
- Request that processing be paused

### 9.5 Right to Data Portability
You have the right to:
- Request your data in a portable format
- Receive your transaction history and account information
- Transfer your data if possible
